# Referrals

**Earn rewards on every bet placed by users you refer**

</div>

---

## Overview

Arena's referral program rewards you for bringing new players to the platform. Once someone uses your referral link, you earn a share of **every bet they place** — win or lose — for as long as they keep playing.

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **Earn** | A percentage of each referred user's bet, on every round they play |
| **Win or Lose** | You earn whether your referrals win or lose |
| **Automatic** | Rewards are paid as part of round settlement |
| **Source** | Paid from Arena's platform share — never from winnings |

## Getting Your Referral Link

Your referral link is generated automatically when you create a profile.

| Step | Action |
|:--|:--|
| **1** | Connect your wallet at [playarena.app](https://playarena.app) |
| **2** | Create a profile from the wallet dropdown |
| **3** | Open your profile and select the **Referrals** tab |
| **4** | Copy your referral code or full link |

Your link looks like:

```
playarena.app/?ref=YOURCODE
```

---

## How Referrals Are Counted

A referral is recorded when a new user:

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **Visits** | Arena via your `?ref=` link |
| **Connects** | Their wallet |
| **Creates** | A profile (this locks in the referral relationship) |

After that, every bet that wallet places contributes to your earnings — there is no expiration.

> Self-referrals and circular referrals are blocked automatically.

---

## How Rewards Are Paid

Rewards are calculated and paid at the **end of each round** in which a referred user placed a bet:

| Step | Action |
|:--|:--|
| **1** | Round settles, vault funds are swept to treasury |
| **2** | Referral rewards are calculated for every referred bet in the round |
| **3** | Rewards are sent directly to each referrer's wallet |
| **4** | Earnings are added to your profile total |

The reward is taken from Arena's **14% platform share**, not from the winnings pool — your referrals' payouts are unaffected by the program.

> Each individual bet generates at most one reward, even if a referrer is referred by another user.

## Tracking Your Earnings

Open your profile and select the **Referrals** tab to see:

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **Referred** | Number of users who joined through your link |
| **Total Earned** | Lifetime SOL earned from referral rewards |
| **Code** | Your personal referral code |
| **Link** | Your full shareable URL |

Your stats also appear on the all-time profit leaderboard — winnings and referral earnings count separately.

## Verifying Rewards

Every referral payout is a real on-chain transaction:

| Step | Action |
|:--|:--|
| **1** | Open your wallet's transaction history |
| **2** | Locate the SOL transfer received during a settled round |
| **3** | Look up the transaction on [Solscan](https://solscan.io) |
| **4** | Verify the sender is the round's treasury wallet |

The [Provably Fair](provably-fair.md) page also lets you cross-reference settled rounds and their treasury activity.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to claim my rewards?

No. Rewards are sent directly to your wallet at the end of each round your referrals play.

### Will my referrals see that I'm earning from them?

Referrals can see that they were referred by you (your wallet), but they don't pay you anything — rewards come entirely from Arena's platform share.

### Is there a cap on how much I can earn?

There is no per-user cap. Rewards continue for as long as your referrals play. (A safety cap exists per-round to protect the platform's other allocations, but it is well above the standard reward amount.)

### What if my referral wins big?

You still earn a reward on their bet — and they keep their winnings in full. The two don't compete.

### Can I refer myself with a second wallet?

No. Self-referrals are detected and blocked. Wallets that exhibit obvious self-referral patterns may have rewards withheld.

### What if a round is cancelled?

Cancelled rounds refund all bets and pay no fees, so no referral reward is generated for that round.
